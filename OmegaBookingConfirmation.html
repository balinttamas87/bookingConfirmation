We do have a full supplier manual containing our delivery requirements however please find below some key points worth checking before attempting to deliver into us.
Please note the below list is not a replacement for reading our Supplier Manual but is intended to support you with some quick easy checks.

• All your cartons/pallets should be labelled with your Booking Reference Number as above.
• All PO’s being delivered must be quoted at time of booking request.
• Please ensure that your driver/carrier has the booking reference as they will be required to quote this at the gatehouse to gain entry to the site.
• If delivering on pallets, these must be Euro’s (1200mm x 800mm).
• Please note that the maximum height for a single pallet of stock is 1.8metres (including the pallet).
• Please note that the maximum weight for a single pallet of stock is 1000kg (including the pallet)
• All items on the delivery must be barcoded.
• No outer cartons can weigh more than 25kg, i.e. one man lift
• No delivery will be reworked by The Hut Group. All Deliveries must comply with the requirements of our Supplier Manual otherwise you will be asked to uplift and rework it
• We cannot accept extra-long trailers >16.5m due to turning circle restrictions onsite.
